Mission


We want to create a platform in which the people of Detroit can easily find the locations of various healthcare locations near them. In addition we want to provide information on the various resources available to them, including: food banks, urgent cares, and Covid-19 testing centers. This platform will hopefully pave the way for future initiatives.That are inspired from geocaching, which aims to provide free reproductive health products around Detroit.

What is Geo Caching?

Geo Caching is a outdoor activity that uses a Global Positioning System on your phone.Other navigation techniques that use hide and seek containers, other also called Geo caches or caches are done at a specific marked location around the world on your map.Thousands of people are doing Geo caching everyday. Some people consdider this a fun game to play, while others use this to get to know there surrondings. Geo caching can also tell you information and the history about a place or area that your at. An example of this would be a library or movie theatre that holds more history and information than what you may have known previously. This is a great activity for when you want to go outside and have something to do or you just want to explore and find treasure just like a treasure hunter. Its a fun activity that anyone can use no matter where you are you never know there might be a geo cache near you.

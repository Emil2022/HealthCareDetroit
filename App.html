This section of the app shows our leaderboard. For every $1 worth of supplies, you earn 10pts towards your score. When your score totals over 1000pts, every $1 spent is worth 30pts. Bring your receipts to the clerk at the drop-site to collect your points. The top 3 participants get a chance to talk about their passion to help others at the local news station.
